What Is Unity Engine?

We look at using Unity Engine in game design and development, but not only that we believe that if used correctly Unity Engine can be used to make cross-platform applications, including filters for Augmented Reality and Virtual Interactions using Virtual Reality.

Our aim is to produce high quality games and applications through the usage of Unity, all assets used by us are licensed to us either directly through the creator or through purchasing the product using official channels. Upon request information can be provided.

If you or your company would like to use or understand how Unity can be used in your day to day running of a company, then we here at Crimson Media Production can assist in this matter.
